# 2. NIC STUDENT & TRAINING AGREEMENT

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

---

## 1. AGREEMENT OVERVIEW

This Agreement governs participation in training programs, courses, certifications, and internships offered by the National Institute of Caregivers (NIC).

By enrolling in any NIC training program, the student agrees to these terms.

---

## 2. ELIGIBILITY

Students must:

* Meet program entry requirements;
* Provide accurate personal and academic information;
* Comply with NIC rules and codes of conduct.

---

## 3. TRAINING STRUCTURE

NIC training may include:

* Online learning
* Physical classes
* Practical sessions
* Internships
* Examinations

NIC reserves the right to modify course delivery formats.

---

## 4. ASSESSMENT & CERTIFICATION

1. Certification is subject to:

   * Attendance
   * Assessment performance
   * Ethical conduct
2. NIC may withhold certification for misconduct or failure to meet standards.
3. Certificates remain the property of NIC and may be revoked.

---

## 5. INTERNSHIPS & PLACEMENTS

1. Internships are educational placements, not employment.
2. NIC is not responsible for stipends, accommodation, or employment outcomes.
3. Host facilities operate independently.

---

## 6. FEES & REFUNDS

1. Training fees are payable as prescribed.
2. Fees are generally non-refundable.
3. Failure to complete training does not entitle a student to refunds.

---

## 7. STUDENT CONDUCT

Students shall:

* Act professionally and ethically;
* Respect clients, instructors, and peers;
* Avoid misconduct, abuse, or falsification of records.

Violations may result in suspension or expulsion.

---

## 8. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

NIC is not liable for:

* Personal injury during training or internship (except as required by law);
* Employment outcomes;
* Third-party actions.

---

## 9. DATA & RECORDS

Student records shall be maintained in accordance with NIC Privacy Policy.

---

## 10. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the **Federal Republic of Nigeria**.

# 3. NIC INSPECTION, COMPLIANCE & SANCTIONS POLICY

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Policy establishes the framework for inspections, compliance enforcement, and sanctions applicable to caregivers and care facilities under NIC oversight.

---

## 2. INSPECTION AUTHORITY

NIC inspectors are authorized to:

* Conduct facility inspections;
* Review documentation;
* Interview staff;
* Assess care practices.

---

## 3. COMPLIANCE RATINGS

Facilities may be classified as:

* **Compliant**
* **Conditionally Compliant**
* **Non-Compliant**
* **Suspended**

---

## 4. SANCTIONS

NIC may impose sanctions including:

1. Written warnings;
2. Corrective action plans;
3. Conditional accreditation;
4. Suspension;
5. Revocation;
6. Public disclosure of status.

---

## 5. URGENT ENFORCEMENT

NIC may immediately suspend accreditation where:

* Client safety is at risk;
* Abuse or neglect is suspected;
* Criminal conduct is indicated.

---
