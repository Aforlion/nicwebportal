# 3. NIC INSPECTION, COMPLIANCE & SANCTIONS POLICY

**Effective Date:** [Insert Date]

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This Policy establishes the framework for inspections, compliance enforcement, and sanctions applicable to caregivers and care facilities under NIC oversight.

---

## 2. INSPECTION AUTHORITY

NIC inspectors are authorized to:

* Conduct facility inspections;
* Review documentation;
* Interview staff;
* Assess care practices.

---

## 3. COMPLIANCE RATINGS

Facilities may be classified as:

* **Compliant**
* **Conditionally Compliant**
* **Non-Compliant**
* **Suspended**

---

## 4. SANCTIONS

NIC may impose sanctions including:

1. Written warnings;
2. Corrective action plans;
3. Conditional accreditation;
4. Suspension;
5. Revocation;
6. Public disclosure of status.

---

## 5. URGENT ENFORCEMENT

NIC may immediately suspend accreditation where:

* Client safety is at risk;
* Abuse or neglect is suspected;
* Criminal conduct is indicated.
